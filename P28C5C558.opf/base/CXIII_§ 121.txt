|na khalv evaṃ draṣṭavyam api tv eṣa eva suvarṇavarṇaḥ tena kālena (4) tena samayena tasya
bhikṣor antike citta pradūṣya kharaṃ vākkarma niścāritaṃtasya (5) karmaṇo vipākena pañcajanmaśatāni
narakeṣu pattvā manuṣyeṣu cotpannaḥ pañca(1)jātiśatāni adūṣy anapakārī śūlam
āropitoyāvad etarhy api tenaiva karma(2)vaśeṇādūṣy anapakārī śūlam ārāpitoyady anena tat
karma kṛtvā (3) nātyayam atyayato deśitaṃ syāt tad eṣa pañcajanmaśatāny ekaikasmiṃ ma(4)hāniraye
pakvaḥ syāt |pañcajanmaśatāni preteṣu pañcajanmaśatāni (5) tiryakṣūpapannaḥ syāt |yad
anena bhagavataś candrasya samyaksambuddhasya śāsane bra(1)hmacaryañ caritaṃ tasya karmaṇo
vipākenaitarhy anena bhagavacchāsane pravrajyā sarvakleśa(2)prahāṇād arhatvaṃ sākṣātkṛtam
iti ||tasmāt tarhi mahārāja ātmano duḥ(3)kham anvicchatā na pareṣām antike cittapradūṣaṇaṃ
kartavyaṃ ||ayaśobhītena ca na(4) pareṣām ayaśo ’nupradeśyaṃ |ātmanaḥ sukham anvicchatā
