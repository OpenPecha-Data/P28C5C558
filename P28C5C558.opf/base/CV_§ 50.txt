(5) [50] atha suvarṇavarṇaḥ kumāraḥ śūlaṃ pṛthivyāṃ pratiṣṭhāpya mānam ālokya mātaram anusmṛtya
tī(1)vramanyudravīkṛtasantāno ’śrūṇi prapātayitum ārabdhaḥ |sāṃprataṃ mātur madviyogaṃ
śru(2)tvā kā ’vasthā bhaviṣyati |yā hi nāma pūrvaṃ yāmārddhamātram api mām apaśyantī parā(3)pīḍāpraveditavatī
sā mayādyaikaputreṇa viyuktā kathaṃ prāṇān dhārayiṣyati |hā (4) kaṣṭam
īdṛśo ’py aham evam adhanyo yan mām āgamya mātā madviyogaduḥkhaṃ pratyanu(5)bhaviṣyatīti
|tatas tair vadhyaghātakaiḥ tat śūlaṃ pṛthivyāṃ nikhātaṃ paraparāṅ ca saṅjalpaṅ kartu(1)m
ārabdhaḥ |ko ’smākaṃ kumāraṃ śūle samāropayati |tatra caikaiko ’pi vyājaṃ kartum ārabdho
(2) yūyam āropayata |mama śirorujātyartham bādhate |mama pṛṣṭhaśūlam mama pārśvaśūla(3)m itina
teṣāṃ kaścid utsahate sma tad akāryaṅ kartuṃ ||
