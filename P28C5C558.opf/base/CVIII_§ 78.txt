[78] tato rājā sthavi(1)rānandaṃ papraccha sthavira kiṃ divākareṇa sārthavāhena sapatnīkena
karma kṛtaṃ |yasya karmaṇo vi(2)pākenāḍhyo mahādhano mahābhogaḥ sasaṃvṛttaḥ |bhagavacchāsane
ca satyadarśanaḥ (3) kṛtaḥ |kāśisundareṇa pracaṇḍena ca bhikṣuṇā kiṃ karma kṛtaṃ yenāḍhyo
mahā(4)dhane mahābhoge kule jāto bhagavacchāsane ca pravrajyā sarvakleśaprahāṇā(5)d arhatvaṃ
sākṣātkṛtaṃ |suvarṇavarṇena ca bhikṣuṇā kiṃ karma kṛtaṃ | yasya karmaṇo vipāke(1) nādya
mahādhane mahābhoge kule jātaḥ |evamabhirupo darśanīyaḥ | prāsādikaḥ sarvā(2)ṅgapratyaṅgopeta
uttaptasuvarṇavarṇayā varṇapuṣkalatayā samanvāgataḥ | sarvajanama(3)nonayanaharaḥ
suvarṇavarṇair vastrair avaguṇṭhitavigrahaḥ |kāyāc cāsya candanaga(4)ndho vāti | mukhāc ca
nīlotpalagandhojanmani cāsya vastravaryaṃ karṇikārakusuma(5)varyaṃ ca patitaṃ | tāni ca vastrāṇi
suvarṇavarṇānievam atyārtha maharddhiko mahānubhāvo bhaga(1)vacchāsane pravrajyā sarvakleśaprahāṇād
arhatvaṃ sākṣātkṛtam iti |
